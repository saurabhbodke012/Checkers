To compile and start up the application:
	mvn compile exec:java
	
To connect to the WebCheckers server:
	localhost:4567
	
To play as more than one player in the same browser on different tabs:
	127.0.0.1:4567 in the first tab
	127.0.0.2:4567 in the second tab
	127.0.0.n:4567 in the nth tab, if desired
